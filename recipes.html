Local Recipes

Learn how to prepare typical dishes from different countries.

Paella, Spain

Paella is a classic Spanish dish that combines seafood, rice, and a mix of vegetables and spices. It is often cooked in a large pan and shared among family and friends.

Ingredients: Rice, seafood (shrimp, mussels, squid), chicken, saffron, bell peppers, peas, tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, lemon.

Instructions: Cook seafood, chicken, and vegetables, then add rice and saffron. Simmer everything together until the rice absorbs the flavors. Serve with lemon wedges.

Sushi, Japan

Sushi is a famous Japanese dish made with vinegared rice and a variety of ingredients, including seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.

Ingredients: Sushi rice, nori (seaweed), fresh fish (salmon, tuna), wasabi, soy sauce, pickled ginger.

Instructions: Cook sushi rice and season with rice vinegar. Place the rice on nori sheets, top with thinly sliced fish, and roll tightly. Serve with wasabi, soy sauce, and ginger.

Tacos, Mexico

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of soft tortillas filled with a variety of fillings such as meat, beans, and vegetables, topped with salsa, cheese, and lime.

Ingredients: Soft corn tortillas, ground beef or chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, salsa, cilantro, lime.

Instructions: Cook the meat with spices. Warm the tortillas, then fill them with the cooked meat and toppings. Serve with salsa, cheese, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
