Site Plan: Travel & Local Cuisine Blog

1. Website Name

Name: Travel & Local Cuisine Blog

Description: This name was chosen to reflect the merging of travel and local cuisine, offering readers an exploration of destinations and authentic dishes from around the world.

2. Website Objective

The website's goal is to provide a space for discovering travel destinations and learning about local cuisine. It will feature sections for destinations, local recipes, and user interactions.

3. Scenarios

  • What is the typical dish in Paris?
  • Where can I find street food in Kyoto?

4. Color Scheme

Primary Color: #4CAF50 (Green - Used for headers and buttons to grab attention.)

Secondary Color: #ffffff (White - Used for backgrounds and content areas for a clean and readable design.)

5. Typography

Main Font: Arial, sans-serif (Used for body content.)

Secondary Font: 'Georgia', serif (Used for headings and subheadings for an elegant touch.)
